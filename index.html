quantum summer

a new world, built from ancestral memories.                                   

quantum summer is a meditative journey through the stars. here on Planet Inkwell, Blackness thrives. the waters of its placid oceans are a site of joy, not trauma. Black fun emanates from the beach, trees, and mountaintops. this is a paradise, shaped by the imagination of its inhabitants. on different parts of the island, you will find 4 sacred sites where Afronauts have left mission logs as a means of communicating with others in the interstitium.
